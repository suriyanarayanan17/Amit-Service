    SSD/ Hard Disk Installations



    SSD and hard disk are the storage devices of your computer, essential for storing all your data, files, programs, and operating system. On the other hand, a hard disk drive (HDD) is known for its affordability, larger storage capacity, and broader compatibility. Depending on your preferences and requirements, you may opt for either an SSD or a hard disk to suit your needs.
